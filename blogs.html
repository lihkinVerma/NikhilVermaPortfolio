I love to write and read

Why I think writing is important in era of video streaming apps?

We hear so much these days about how video is taking over the internet. People have shorter attention spans, and most of us would prefer to watch a video than to read written text. And that's exactly my concern, reading and writing involve more cognitive ability of humans compared to video. Your blog is the place where you can express and test ideas and refine your message before you commit those ideas to text.

What do I read and write?

Most of my reading is academic but apart from that I guess I like Non-fiction. "Connect the dots", "The power of HABITS", "Storyworthy", "Factfulness", "Light from many lamps", "Ignited Minds", "The white Tiger" are some of names coming to my mind.
Interesting is that I write as well. Most of my writings are technical blogs about day-to-day small experiments or projects I do. In case I come across some (new to me) tech/algo, I try to document it with me for rest of the time. At times, I convert the lectures that I take at some workshops, to storyworthy notes. You could catch my writings at my medium handle [lih-verma].
